LuminaGuard

LuminaGuard is an AI-driven adaptive lighting solution enhancing comfort, productivity, and well-being. It adjusts screen brightness, color temperature, and integrates with smart lighting systems to create harmonized environments. Under the hood, LuminaGuard uses reinforcement learning and ML-driven adaptation to refine conditions over time.

Key Features

  • Real-time brightness and color adjustments.
  • Circadian-based scheduling and temperature changes.
  • Integration with popular IoT lighting ecosystems.
  • Adaptive ML engine for personalized lighting patterns.
