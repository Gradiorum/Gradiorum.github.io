Discord Bots

Gradiorum’s Discord integrations bring innovative capabilities straight into your community servers.

GradiorumMP3

GradiorumMP3 is a music manager bot that can play MP3 files, handle playlists, queue management, skipping, pausing, and resuming tracks. It transforms your Discord server into a social hub of shared listening experiences.

Polishadow

Polishadow monitors politicians’ stock trades and uses synthetic data insights to inform subscribers of potential insider patterns. It can paper trade strategies, issue alerts, and even offer subscription tiers so you always stay informed on the latest Wall Street moves.

Stay tuned for more bots as we continue to expand our Discord ecosystem.
