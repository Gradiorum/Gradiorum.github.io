Scaling Synthetic Data for Behavioral Health Classification

Authors: William Stigall, Md Abdullah Al Hafiz Khan

We investigate synthetic data generation using large language models (LLMs) to enrich behavioral health classification datasets. Topics include prompt engineering, adversarial examples, and quality control at scale.
